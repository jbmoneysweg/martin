Frequently asked questions

What does self-management entail?
Your IT team will oversee deployment and ongoing management of our security tools. For your security, password management is always self-managed.
Will you help with password migration?
We provide tips and resources to help faciliate your password migration. If you want more hands-on assistance, we provide password migration services at an hourly rate.
How long does implementation typically take?
Schedule 1 week for each technology deployed. On average it will take 3 weeks for password, email, and device protection.
Do you provide compliance for SOC, ISO, NIST, HIPAA, etc.?
Our technical controls provide the necessary bedrock for most security frameworks. Our partner network can help with security auditing and compliance as you grow.
What if I have additional cybersecurity needs specific to my industry?
We work with a variety of high-risk industries such as fintech, infrastructure, government, and healthcare. Let us know your concerns to see if we can accommodate your requirements.
I already have some protection, can I unbundle your services?
We advise bundling for most organizations. However, it is possible to unbundle specific technologies. Reach out to us for a discussion on your unique needs.
